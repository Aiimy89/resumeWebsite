001 : Blood Donating Database System

As part of a group project, we developed a comprehensive database system for a blood donating event. The system efficiently manages patient registrations and stores crucial information, including user IDs, patient IDs, sickness IDs, hospital IDs, and history IDs. This system simplifies the process of patient registration and facilitates easy retrieval of patient information, enhancing overall efficiency in healthcare services.
